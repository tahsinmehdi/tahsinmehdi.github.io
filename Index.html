I am research economist at Statistics Canada, and former labour economist at the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD). I have a PhD in economics from the University of Portsmouth (2020).

My research interests include econometrics, labour economics, poverty, inequality, and multidimensional well-being. I have published papers in leading journals such as the Economics Letters, Canadian Public Policy, Canadian Tax Journal, Social Indicators Research, Child Indicators Research, Econometrics, and Communications in Statistics - Theory and Methods.
